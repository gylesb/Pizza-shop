About the pizzas we serve here:

"The Regular" - Your basic cheese and tomato sauce pizza. Nothing too exciting.
"The Vegan" - For all you vegans out there! A bunch of veggies and tomato sauce...no cheese here!
"The BEAST" - The complete opposite of "The Vegan". Basically a heart attack with bread.
"The Yawka" - Your typical New York style Pizza with cheese and pepperoni. Greasy, cheap, fulfilling. YUM!
"The Ditka" - Rounding off we have a Chicago style pizza named after famed Chicago Bears coach, Mike Ditka. Deep dish with TONS of tomato sauce.
